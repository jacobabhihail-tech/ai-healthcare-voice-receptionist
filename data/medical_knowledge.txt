# Common Cold

Symptoms:
- Sneezing
- Runny nose
- Mild fever
- Cough

Advice:
Drink fluids, get rest, and consult a GP if symptoms worsen.

----------------------------------------------------

# Fever

Symptoms:
- Temperature above 38°C
- Chills
- Sweating

Advice:
Stay hydrated and seek medical advice if fever lasts more than 3 days.

----------------------------------------------------

# Headache

Symptoms:
- Pain in head
- Pressure
- Sensitivity to light

Advice:
Rest, stay hydrated, and consult a healthcare professional if severe.

----------------------------------------------------

# Diabetes

Symptoms:
- Increased thirst
- Frequent urination
- Fatigue

Advice:
Monitor blood sugar regularly and consult your doctor.

----------------------------------------------------

# Chest Pain

Symptoms:
- Pressure in chest
- Pain while breathing
- Tightness

Advice:
Seek immediate emergency medical attention.
